>> Aliens Want Our Mascot?!

Role: Producer and programmer
Tools used: C#, Unity, PlasticSCM
Team Size: 6
Timeline: 11 weeks

Aliens Want Our Mascot?! is a tower defense game about a high school that bands together to protect their school mascot from alien invaders. As part of MassDigi's 2023 Summer Innovation Program, my team and I developed this game from concept to launch in 11 weeks. Working with such a small team and short timeframe allowed me to gain experience programming a variety of game systems, as well as experience the entire game development process of prototyping, playtesting, and iterating to meet tight deadlines.
